Questions, answered

Is my journal really private?

Yes. Ramble stores everything on your device, encrypted with AES-256. There’s no account and no server that receives your entries, so there is nothing for us to read, sell, or lose.

Does it work offline?

The core experience — recording, transcription, moods, archive, insights — works fully offline. The only feature that reaches the internet is the optional “song of the moment” search, and only when you use it.

How does transcription work?

Transcription runs on your device using your operating system’s built-in speech recognition. Accuracy depends on your language, accent, and device, and improves over time with the platform.

What happens if I lose or change my phone?

Create an encrypted, password-protected backup from Settings and keep it somewhere safe (your Files, iCloud Drive, or Google Drive). You can restore it on any device — and because the format is shared, a backup from iPhone opens on Android and vice-versa.

Is Ramble free?

The core app is free. An optional “Ramble Plus” unlocks extra themes, deeper personalization, and more — but backing up and exporting your own data is always free.

Which languages are supported?

The interface is localized into 30 languages, including English and Turkish.
